I am a PhD Candidate in the Ecology, Evolution, and Behavior program at the University of Texas at Austin in the [Meyers lab](http://www.bio.utexas.edu/). My research interests include the impacts of ubranization on wildlife health, host-parasite interactions, human-wildlife interactions, and the implications this may have for spread of zoonotic diseases. I am interested in using a combination of field, lab, and quantitative techniquies to understand these complex topics from an ecological and epidemiologal standpoint.
